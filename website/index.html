My visual personal statement

My name is Dominika and I believe in multi-dimensionality. Just as no man is an island, no skill, talent or expertise stands on its own. What may seem as an irrelevant hobby at first can add a new layer of perspective, hence complementing our point of view. This portfolio is, hopefully, a practical demonstration of my sentiment.
In the process of new encounters and experiences, I realised I wanted to combine my current degree with a creative outlet which led me to creating this website.
